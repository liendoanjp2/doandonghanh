Announcing Virtual Sinh Hoạt 2020!

Theme: "Jesus Christ in the Eucharist: The Source And Summit"


We can probably all agree that 2020 has been one crazy year. Yet in the chaos, what has not changed is God’s love for us and His desire for us to unite with Him. All the activities during virtual sinh hoạt serves to build each other up and bring us closer to Jesus’ loving embrace. To be honest, virtual sinh hoạt will never match the in-person experience. Yet it is a blessing that despite having to be far apart, we are still in this together; that technology innovation means we can still be with each other online. We look foreward for các em to join us in virtual sinh hoạt this year!
